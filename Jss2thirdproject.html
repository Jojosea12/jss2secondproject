About My Favourite Food

My favourite food is Jollof Rice and Chicken. It is one of the most popular meals in Nigeria. I enjoy eating it during birthdays, Christmas celebrations, weddings, and family gatherings.

Why I Love It

  • It tastes delicious.
  • It has a beautiful aroma.
  • It is colourful and attractive.
  • It gives energy.
  • I enjoy sharing it with my family.
